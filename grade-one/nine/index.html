Grade One Kanji: 九

九

How to read in Japanese

Onyomi
シチ shichi
Kunyomi
なな nana
ななつ nanatsu
なの nano
Lecture Video

more examples

シチ shichi
七五三 shichigosann an event to celebrate the growth of children. generally, boys are celebrated at 3 and 5 years old, girls are celebrated at 3 and 7 years old
七福神 shichifukujinn The Seven Deities of Good Luck (Good Fortune)
七転八倒 shittenbattou (shichitenbattou) (four-character idiom) the direct translation is "falling or turning seven times and toppling eight times"; writhing in pain
なな nana
七癖 nanakuse subconscious habits
七転び八起き nanakorobiyaoki the direct translation is "falling seven times but waking up eight times"; even after failing many times, trying again without giving up
七不思議 nanafushigi seven mysterious things being told in a certain area or region
Special Reading
七夕 tanabata The Star Festival celebrated on July 7
