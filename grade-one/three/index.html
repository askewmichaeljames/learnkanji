Grade One Kanji: 三

三

How to read in Japanese
サン - sann
み - mi
みつ - mitsu
みっつ - mittsu
Lecture Video
“ three (三), three days (三日), crescent moon (三日月), three (三つ) ”
Examples

三

sann

three

三日

mikka

three days

三日月

mikaduki

crescent moon

三つ

mittsu

three

more examples

サン sann
三角 sankaku triangle
三寒四温 sankanshionn (four-character idiom) the climate of Winter to early Spring; repeating periods of 3 cold days and 4 warm days
三原色 sangenshoku three primary colors
三時 sanji three o'clock
三分 sanpunn three minutes
み mi
三毛猫 mikeneko calico cat; cat with three colors of fur
みつ mitsu
三つ子 mitsugo triplets, triplet
三日坊主 mikkabouzu (four-character idiom) get bored quickly and not last long
Special Reading
三味線 shamisenn Shamisen, Japanese guitar, Japanese traditional instrumental
