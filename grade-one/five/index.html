Grade One Kanji: 五

五

How to read in Japanese
ゴ - go
イツ - itsu
いつつ - itsutsu
Lecture Video
“ five (五), five days (五日), five (五つ) ”
Examples

五

go

five

五日

itsuka

five days

五つ

itsutsu

five

more examples

ゴ go
五感 gokann the five senses; sight, hearing, taste, smell, and touch
五色 goshiki, goshoku (syoku) five colors as blue, red, yellow, white, and black
五十歩百歩 gojippohyappo (idiom derived from historical events) although different, almost the same
五十音 gojuuonn (Japanese syllabary) the fifty sounds of a, ka, sa, ta, na, ha, ma, ya, ra, and wa with five characters each
五里霧中 gorimuchuu (idiom derived from historical events) to not know the prospects of things or what to do with them
五分五分 gobugobu there is almost no difference
五輪 gorinn Olympics
Special Reading
五月 satsuki May of the lunar calendar
五月雨 samidare A continuous rain falling around May of the lunar calendar,rainy season
