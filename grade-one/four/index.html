Grade One Kanji: 四

四

How to read in Japanese
シ - shi (si)
よ - yo
よつ - yotsu
よっつ - yottsu
よん - yonn
Lecture Video
“ four (四), four people (四人), four days (四日), four (四つ), April (四月) ”
Examples

四

yonn

four

四人

yoninn

four people

四日

yokka

four days

四つ

yottsu

four

四月

shigatsu

April

more examples

シ shi
四角 shikaku square
四季 shiki four seasons; four seasons of Spring, Summer, Fall, and Winter
四苦八苦 shikuhakku (four-character idiom) to suffer very much; terrible suffering (originally from Buddhism)
四捨五入 shishagonyuu rounding off
四方 shihou all sides; every direction; directions of north, south, west, and east
四面楚歌 shimensoka (four-character idiom) surrounded by enemies or opponents, with no allies or supporters
四六時中 shirokujichuu (four-character idiom) all day; day and night; around-the-clock
よつ yotsu
四つ角 yotsukado crossroads; a place where two roads meet
