Grade One Kanji: 一

一

How to read in Japanese
イチ - ichi
イツ - itsu
ひと - hito
ひとつ - hitotsu
Lecture Video
“ one (一), lifetime (一生), one person (一人), one (一つ) ”
Examples

一

ichi

one

一生

isshou

lifetime

一人

hitori

one person

一つ

hitotsu

one

more examples

イチ ichi
一位 ichii first place, first rank, unit's position
一時 ichiji one o'clock, short period of time
一大事 ichidaiji big thing, serious affair
一番 ichiban number one, best
一日 ichinichi one day, whole day
一人前 ichininmae for one person, adult, full manhood
イツ itsu
一等 ittou first class, first rank, best, most
一時 ittoki a time, moment, short period of time, once, some time ago
一般 ippan general, common, ordinary, normal, usual, customary, unremarkable, habitual, pompier, rife, unexceptional, common-or-garden, rank-and-file
ひと hito
一雨 hitoame a shower, a rainfall
一安心 hitoanshinn a sense of relief, feeling of relief
一時 hitotoki short period of time, some time ago, formerly
Special Reading
一日 tsuitachi first day of month
