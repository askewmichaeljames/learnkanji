Grade One Kanji: 十

十

How to read in Japanese

Onyomi
ジュウ juu (zyuu)
ジッ jixtsu (zixtu)
Kunyomi
とお too
と to
Lecture Video
“ 10 (十), Eight or nine out of ten; almost; mostly (十中八九), ten days (十日), different strokes for different folks (十人十色) ”
Examples

十

juu

10

十中八九

jicchuuhakku

eight or nine out of ten; almost; mostly

十日

tooka

ten days

十人十色

juunintoiro

different strokes for different folks

more examples

ジュウ juu
十人 juuninn ten people
十二分 juunibunn enough; more than 100% (十分 is 100%)
十年一日 juunennichijitsu (four-character idiom) there are no changes to the state for a long time
十年一昔 juunenhitomukashi (four-character idiom) after 10 years, the appearance of the world will change completely, so people will feel like ten years ago is the distant past
十分 juubunn sufficient; satisfactory; perfect (adj), fully; plenty; in full (adv), plenty; full; sufficiency; division into ten (n)
　
ジッ jixtsu
十指 jisshi 10 fingers
十進法 jisshinhou decimal system
とお too
十 too ten (10)
Special Reading
二十、二十歳 hatachi twenty years old
二十日 hatsuka the 20th of the month
