Grade One Kanji: 六

六

How to read in Japanese
ロク - roku
リク - riku
む - mu
むい - mui
むつ - mutsu
むっつ - muttsu
Lecture Video
“ six (六), six days (六日), six (六つ) ”
Examples

六

roku

six

六日

muika

six days

六つ

muttsu

six

more examples

ロク roku
六歳(六才) rokusai 6 years old
六十 rokujuu sixty
六人 rokuninn 6 people
リク riku
六書 rikusho One of the kanji classification methods such as hieroglyphs (象形), gestures (指事), meditations (会意), voices (形声), transfers (転注), temporary borrowing (仮借)
む mu
二四六九士 nishimukusamurai A mnemonic for remembering the months without 31 days. The sound is the same as the meaning of "a samurai is facing west (西向く侍(士))". 二月 = Feb.,四月 = Apr., 六月 = Jun., 九月 = Sep. "Samurai" is also written as 士, and it looks like 十 + 一 = 十一 = Nov.
むい mui
六日 muika six days, sixth day of the month
むつ mutsu
六つ子 mutsugo sextuplets, six chidren born at one birth
