Grade Two Kanji: 冬

冬

How to read in Japanese

Onyomi
トウ tou
Kunyomi
ふゆ fuyu
Lecture Video
“ Winter (冬), Winter Solstice (冬至) ”
Examples

冬

fuyu

Winter

冬至

touji

Winter Solstice

more examples

トウ tou
冬季 touki Winter season
冬期 touki Winter period
冬眠 touminn hibernation; Winter sleep
初冬 shotou (hatsufuyu) early Winter
暖冬 dantou warm Winter; mild Winter
ふゆ fuyu
冬着 fuyugi Winter clothes
冬木立 fuyukodachi standing tree with fallen leaves in Winter
冬日 fuyubi Winter sunshine; Day when the temperature of the day is below 0 degrees Celsius
冬物 fuyumono Winter goods
真冬 mafuyu midwinter
