Grade Two Kanji: 西

西

How to read in Japanese

Onyomi
サイ sai
セイ sei
Kunyomi
にし nishi
Lecture Video
“ west (西), northwest (北西), Kansai (関西) ”
Examples

西

nishi

west

北西

hokusei

northwest

関西

kansai

Kansai

(a region of Japan)

more examples

サイ sai
西下 saika going to Kansai area (west) from Tokyo (east)
西国 saigoku (saikoku) west country
　
セイ sei
西南 seinann southwest
西部劇 seibugeki Movies or TV dramas depicting the pioneering era of the Western US
西方 seihou western direction
西北 seihoku northwest
西洋 seiyou European and American countries
西暦 seireki Anno Domini (A.D.)
にし nishi
西半球 nishihankyuu Western Hemisphere; Occident
西日 nishibi West sun and its light; evening sun
Special Reading
西瓜 suika watermelon
